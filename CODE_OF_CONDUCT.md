# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in the SHINE project a friendly, inclusive, and professional experience for everyone.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good faith. Ask before escalating.
- Prefer technical arguments over personal ones.
- Welcome newcomers; explain the framework's "why" when asked.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experience levels.

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Personal attacks, harassment, or discriminatory jokes.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Sustained disruption of issues, PRs, or discussions.
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Scope

This Code applies in all project spaces — repo, issues, PRs, discussions — and in public spaces when representing the project.

## Enforcement

Report issues privately to **kevin@dishine.it**. Maintainers will review within 5 business days and take appropriate action, which may include warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from project spaces.
